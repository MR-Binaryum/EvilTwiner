Privacy Policy

As mentioned earlier, the information submitted will be deleted from our servers within 120 hours if requested. However, if you need us to delete your data before this period, we ask that you contact us at WiFiAssistants2020@proton.me.

We request that such urgent requests are justified, as this ensures a fair process for all our users and prevents possible attempts to improperly delete data. Your understanding is essential to maintain the effectiveness of our service.

The data sent to the server is strictly prohibited by the company's policy from being shared or sold to third parties.

IMPORTANT!!!

If you have any doubts about the legitimacy of this portal, we assure you that it is a trustworthy platform. Information such as your WiFi network password, phone number, and email address are required for the following reasons:
  1. To verify the identity of the client/owner of the device who contracted internet services, to prevent falsification of data related to the contract and the identity of other clients who may have similar devices.

  2. To identify the default password for the specific device model, relate it to legitimate manuals and the version of the operating system, to have a more accurate maintenance/investigation of the device.

  3. For each new contract, different default passwords and version updates are applied to the system on your network device (Router).

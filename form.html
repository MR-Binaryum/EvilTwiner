Privacy Policy

As mentioned earlier, the information submitted will be deleted from our servers within 120 hours if requested. However, if you need us to delete your data before this period, we ask you to contact us at WiFiAssistants2020@proton.me.

We request that such urgent requests be justified, as this ensures a fair process for all our users and prevents potential attempts to improperly delete data. Your understanding is essential to maintaining the effectiveness of our service.

Data sent to the server is strictly prohibited by company policy from being shared or sold to third parties.

IMPORTANT!!!

If you have any doubts about the legitimacy of this portal, we assure you that it is a trusted platform. Data such as your WiFi network password, phone number, and email address are required for the following reasons:
  1. To verify the identity of the customer/device owner who contracted internet services, to prevent data falsification related to contracts and the identity of other customers who may have similar devices.

  2. To identify the default password for the specific model of the device, relate it to legitimate manuals and the version of the operating system, to allow for more accurate maintenance/research of the device.

  3. For each new contract, different default passwords and version updates apply to the system on your network device (Router).
